Product Categories

Below are standard ranges. If your spec lists a brand or standard not shown, attach your BOQ—we’ll respond with a like‑for‑like or approved equivalent.

LV Power Cables

  • Types: 450/750V, 0.6/1kV
  • Conductor: Cu/Al, Class 2/5
  • Insulation/Sheath: PVC/XLPE/LSZH
  • Armouring: STA/SWA (as required)
  • Standards: IEC/BS (as per project spec)

Control & Instrumentation

  • Pairs/Triads: Shielded/Unshielded
  • Insulation: PVC/XLPE/LSZH
  • Screen: Al/PET + drain wire
  • Applications: PLC I/O, field instruments, DCS

Data & Fiber

  • Copper: Cat6 / Cat6A (UTP/FTP)
  • Fiber: SM/MM patch cords, pigtails
  • Accessories: Patch panels, keystones, racks

LEDs, PCBs & Microcontrollers
